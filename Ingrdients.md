# **Chocolete Cake**

-----------------

## Ingredients

- #### 3 *eggs*

- #### 1 1/4 *cups of powdered sugar*

- #### 1 *cup flour* 

- #### 1/2 *cup cocoa*

- #### 1/2 *level tsp backing power*

- #### *a pinch of mitha soda*

- #### 3/4 *cup oil*

- #### 1 *tsp vanilla essence*
